# Using BobikA5

## Overview

BobikA5 is an untethered bypass tool for compatible iOS versions from 7.0 to 10.3.4

::: warning
While BobikA5 does indeed work, it's not recommended to use it. Its device/iOS version support is extremely spotty, and even (allegedly) supported devices have had trouble getting activated with the tool in the past.

The developer has also been known to steal code and break licenses on other tools. When possible, use <a href="using-hacktiv8.html">hacktiv8</a> instead.
:::

## Prerequisites

### What You'll Need

A computer running Windows with the following installed:

- [BobikA5](https://github.com/bablaerrr/bobikA5/releases/latest/download/net48.zip)

**Warning:** Back up any important data before proceeding. This process may reset your device or cause data loss.

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Run BobikA5

Download and extract BobikA5 to an easily accessible location on your computer. Install drivers if needed using the `install this if u dont have drivers.msi` file, and run `BobikA5.exe`.

### Step 2: Connect your device to your computer

Use a USB cable to connect your device to your computer.

### Step 3: Check compatibility

Once connected, click the `Check Device Compatibility` button. If your device is compatible, continue to Step 4.

**Note:** If your device comes up as incompatible (very common), try [deleting setup.app](deleting-setupapp.html) to bypass your device on your current iOS version, or update and proceed to [using hacktiv8](using-hacktiv8.html) to bypass your device.

### Step 4: Bypass

Click the `Activate Your Device` button and wait for the device to reboot 2 times. After this, you should be able to proceed with the setup.
