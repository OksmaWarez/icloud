# Using Hackt1vator

## Overview

Hackt1vator is an untethered bypass tool for Checkm8-compatible devices and A12+ up to iOS 26.1

## Prerequisites

### What You'll Need

A computer running Windows or MacOS with the following installed:

- [Hackt1vator](https://hackt1vator.com/downloads/)

**Warning:** Back up any important data before proceeding. This process may reset your device or cause data loss.

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Run Hackt1vator

Download and extract Hackt1vator to an easily accessible location on your computer and run your platform's equivalent of `Hackt1vator`.

### Step 2: Connect your device to your computer

Use a USB cable to connect your device to your computer.

### Step 3: Register

Join the [Hackt1vator Discord Server, and register your device in the register channel.](https://discord.com/servers/hackt1vator-1116502617510641676)

### Step 3: Bypass

Depending on the device, in the Activation tab, click either A12+ Hello Activation or Hello Activation 12-18/8-10.
